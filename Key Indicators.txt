People with annual salary less than or equal to 300000 tend to default more. so they should take loan for longer term


People with 10+ years experience default most

people living on Rent or have home mortgage default most

DEBT Consolidation has most defaulters. Less Income and more rotational balance default more. Higher DTI is indicator

highest defaulter are in CA, FL, NY


Key Indicators:

1. Salary with respect to Time Period. Less salary applied for less time defaults
2. High year of experience with respect to DTI.
3. High loan amount and type of home stay
4. Loan taken for debt consolidation
5. Loan applicants applied from CA, FL and NY.
